Mini Hair Straightener Brush

Description:

A practical brush that dries and styles hair easily to get smooth and shiny hair. It contains an on / off button with a light indicating the level of heat.

The heat settings have two levels: the first level is 180 Celsius (350 Fahrenheit), the second level is 210 Celsius (410 Fahrenheit), and you can choose between them according to the appropriate for your hair type..

It features an automatic safety system as the device turns off automatically after 60 minutes of use.

Designed with a built-in heater and nanometer comb to give you the best results without damaging your hair. It is small in size and easy to use
